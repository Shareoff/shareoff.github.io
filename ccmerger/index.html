CC Merger Tool

A tool for Rhythm Doctor level creators that takes multiple custom characters and merges them into one.

Click the button below to add a custom character to be merged.
For each custom character you'll be asked to provide a .json and a spritesheet.
Once you've added at least two characters, the option to merge your CCs will appear.
Please feel free to ping/DM Shareoff for any issues or problems!
